Tutorial 002

In the second tutorial we present to you, there are 5 elements arranged vertically: Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, Yoda, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and R2-D2.

In this exercise, you need to select Han Solo and Obi-Wan Kenobi.

Use the ↑ and ↓ keys to navigate to the element you want to select (or deselect), then press the space bar.
